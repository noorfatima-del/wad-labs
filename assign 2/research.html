Research

Research in the department is carried out in areas of faculty interest through research groups.

Human Information Interaction

Investigates all aspects of information usage by humans including behavior, interaction techniques, and retrieval frameworks. HCI research explores how users engage with computer systems, aiming to design more intuitive, efficient, and user-friendly interfaces. This research enhances user experience and drives the development of accessible technologies that cater to diverse populations.

Knowledge Engineering

Focuses on data analysis using mining algorithms to extract contextual knowledge. It includes methodologies and tools for acquiring, representing, and managing knowledge, exploring ontologies, rule-based systems, and semantic networks to simulate expert decision-making in domains like healthcare and finance.

Networking and Communication

Explores networking, distributed systems, security, privacy, IoT, smart cities, and cloud computing. Research aims to improve speed, reliability, and security of data transmission, shaping the future of communication protocols, wireless technologies, and global connectivity infrastructure.
