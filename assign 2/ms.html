MS-IST

The objective of the MS-IST programme is to train students to initiate research work. The duration of the MS-IST programme is two years (4 semesters).

Objectives

In today's Information Society, expertise in all forms of information is required for progress in science, business, and education. The main aim of MS programme in Information Science & Technology (IST) is to design and develop information-related applications using information technology by understanding the relationship between digital information, people, and technology. The MS programme intends to bring together Computer Science and a collaborative environment of how people and society interact with digital information.

MS (Information Science & Technology) - Part Time

Quaid-i-Azam University is keen to serve the community and is providing an opportunity to those who are working in related fields and may not be able to attend a full-time postgraduate programme. Classes will be held in the evening after 5 PM. By getting admission in the programme, applicants will not only improve their qualifications but will be equipped with the latest knowledge and techniques in the field of information studies.

Eligibility

Students must have completed 16 years of education, either BS (4 years) or MSc in computing-related disciplines.

Details about Admissions

Programme Structure

MS programme consists of 30 credit hours. Each course is of 3 credit hours and there are 2 compulsory courses and a number of optional courses.

MS (IST) programme offers two options:

  • 24 credit hours for coursework and 6 credit hours for project/thesis
  • 30 credit hours for coursework only
