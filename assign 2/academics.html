ACADEMICS

The department offers PhD, MPhil, MS (IST), M.Sc. and BS degrees
Each degree programme has a specific objective and focus.
The course of study and syllabi for each degree is updated and is inline its objectives.

Academics

PhD is the highest degree awarded and the most rewarding academic experience. Students work closely with faculty, conducting original research and solving challenging problems. (More Details)

The M.Phil program trains students for research. It's a 2-year program with 50 credit hours: 24 for coursework and 26 for research. (More Details)

The MS in IST focuses on designing and developing IT solutions while understanding digital information, people, and technology. (More Details)

The MS Data Science program equips students with data science tools like programming, analytics, visualization, and big data, preparing them for global markets or PhD (More Details)

The BS program provides balanced knowledge in Computer Science. Students specialize in areas like:

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Software Engineering
  • Data Science
  • Human Centered Computing
  • Platform Based Development
  • Networks and Security
  • Information Management
