Research

Research in the department is carried out in areas of faculty interest through research groups.

Human Information Interaction

Investigates all aspects of information usage by humans including behavior, interaction techniques, and retrieval frameworks. Providing research on Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) plays a crucial role in improving the way people interact with technology.HCI research explores how users engage with computer systems, aiming to design more intuitive, efficient, and user-friendly interfaces. By studying user behavior, cognitive psychology, ergonomics, and design principles, HCI researchers identify challenges in existing systems and propose innovative solutions. This research not only enhances user experience but also drives the development of accessible technologies that cater to diverse populations. As technology becomes more integrated into daily life, ongoing research in HCI remains essential to ensure that digital tools are both effective and inclusive.

Knowledge Engineering

Focuses on data analysis using mining algorithms to extract contextual knowledge for decision-making in systems architecture and services.Knowledge engineering is a critical area of research within artificial intelligence that focuses on creating systems capable of simulating human expertise and decision-making. It involves the development of methodologies and tools for acquiring, representing, and managing knowledge in a structured format that machines can process. Research in knowledge engineering explores techniques such as ontologies, rule-based systems, and semantic networks to capture domain-specific information effectively. It also addresses challenges related to knowledge acquisition, validation, and maintenance, ensuring the reliability and adaptability of intelligent systems. By advancing knowledge engineering, researchers contribute to the development of expert systems, intelligent agents, and decision support tools used across various industries, including healthcare, finance, and education.

Networking and Communication

This group explores applied research in networking, distributed systems, security, privacy, IoT, smart cities, cloud computing, and more.Providing research on network and communication is essential in advancing modern technology and enhancing the efficiency of data transmission. This field focuses on studying how data is exchanged between devices, networks, and systems, aiming to improve speed, reliability, and security. Research in this area contributes to the development of new communication protocols, wireless technologies, and internet infrastructure, which are critical for supporting growing demands such as cloud computing, IoT, and 5G networks. By exploring emerging trends and addressing existing challenges, researchers play a key role in shaping the future of global connectivity and enabling seamless communication across the world.
